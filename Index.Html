Hello! My name is Yonathan Mendenhall, and I’m a 24-year-old currently in my second year of school, majoring in Computer Science. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by technology and its endless potential.

At the age of 14, I embarked on my first project: an online simulation game. Using MariaDB, I managed user information such as usernames and passwords, which ignited my passion for backend development and data management.

When I was 15, I started my own small business unlocking and jailbreaking iPhones — a popular trend at the time. This experience gave me invaluable technical skills and an early taste of entrepreneurship.

My professional journey began with an associate position as a Copy Center Tech at Keystone Property Management. This role changed my perspective on the corporate world, inspiring me to learn more and strive for growth and development within the tech field.

Today, I am passionate about technology and eager to grow within a company that values innovation and professional development. My ultimate goal is to become a Data Analyst, where I can combine my love for data and problem-solving into a fulfilling career.
